FAQ

Common questions about AI automation consulting

What does an AI automation consultant actually do?

I map your manual business processes, identify which steps can be automated, then build workflows using n8n, Make.com, or Zapier — integrating AI models (OpenAI, LangChain) to handle decisions, classification, and content generation automatically. The result is a process that runs 24/7 without human intervention.

How quickly can you deliver an automation project?

Simple 2–3 step automations are typically delivered in 2–5 business days. Complex multi-branch workflows with AI integrations take 1–3 weeks depending on the number of tools involved and testing requirements.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you?

No. I handle all the technical implementation. You describe your current process and desired outcome — I translate that into a working automation, test it, document it, and hand it over with a walkthrough video.

What's the difference between n8n, Make.com, and Zapier?

n8n is open-source and self-hostable — ideal for complex workflows and eliminating per-task costs. Make.com (formerly Integromat) has the most powerful visual scenario builder for multi-step logic. Zapier is the simplest and most widely supported but most expensive at scale. I recommend the right tool based on your technical setup, budget, and workflow complexity.

Which countries do you serve?

I provide remote AI automation consulting services to clients in the USA (California, New York, Texas, Florida), United Kingdom, Germany, and Australia. All work is done asynchronously or via video call to accommodate time zones.
