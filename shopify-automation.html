FAQ

Shopify automation questions

Do you build custom Shopify apps?

For most automation use cases, I use n8n, Make.com, or Zapier connected to the Shopify API — which is faster and cheaper than building a custom Shopify app. For requirements that genuinely need an embedded Shopify app (admin extensions, checkout customizations), I can build that with FastAPI backend and Shopify's Polaris framework.

Can you build an AI chatbot for my Shopify store?

Yes. I build OpenAI-powered chat widgets that integrate with your Shopify store — answering product questions, checking order status via the Shopify API, handling returns, and qualifying leads. The chatbot is trained on your product catalog, policies, and FAQs, and escalates to human support when needed.

How do you connect Shopify to my CRM?

Via n8n or Make.com using Shopify webhooks and the CRM's API. New orders, customers, and product events trigger automatic record creation and updates in HubSpot, Klaviyo, Pipedrive, or whichever CRM you use. Field mapping, deduplication, and error handling are all included.
