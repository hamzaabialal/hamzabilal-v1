FAQ

Questions about Make.com automation

What is Make.com (formerly Integromat)?

Make.com is a visual automation platform with one of the most powerful scenario builders available. It supports complex conditional routing, iterators, aggregators, and error handlers — making it ideal for multi-step workflows that would be complex to build in Zapier. It's cloud-hosted and priced by operation count, not task count.

Make.com vs Zapier vs n8n — which is best?

Make.com wins on visual complexity and flexibility for cloud workflows. Zapier wins on ease of use and app coverage. n8n wins on cost at scale (self-hosted) and custom code capabilities. I recommend the right tool after understanding your specific workflow, team, and budget — often I combine them in the same architecture.

Can you integrate Make.com with Airtable?

Yes — Make.com's Airtable integration is one of the best in the industry, supporting record creation, updates, searches, and file uploads. I've used it to build investigation trackers, CRM supplements, inventory systems, and content pipelines — all synced automatically.

How much does a Make.com project cost?

Simple Make.com scenarios (3–5 modules) typically cost $250–$500. Complex multi-branch scenarios with AI modules, error handling, and Airtable integration run $600–$2,000+. All work is fixed-price with a clear scope upfront.
