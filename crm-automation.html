FAQ

CRM automation questions

Do I need to switch CRMs to get better automation?

No. I work with your existing CRM and add automation on top of it — using n8n, Make.com, or Zapier to extend what it can do natively. The only time a CRM switch makes sense is if your current tool fundamentally can't support your workflow, and I'll tell you honestly if that's the case.

Can you automate HubSpot workflows with AI?

Yes. I integrate OpenAI into HubSpot workflows via n8n — so HubSpot triggers (new contact, stage change, form submit) invoke AI scoring, draft personalized emails, classify intent, and update properties automatically. HubSpot's native AI features are also available and I incorporate those where they fit.

What's the ROI of CRM automation?

The most common results I see: faster lead response time (from hours to minutes), 30–60% reduction in manual data entry, more consistent follow-up sequences, and higher pipeline visibility. Most clients recover the project cost within 30–60 days from productivity savings and improved conversion rates.
